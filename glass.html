Glass Cleaner

What is True Glass?

"True Glass" glass cleaning and polishing liquid is characterized by high quality, which makes it effective in removing dirt and stains from glass surfaces. It contains special ingredients that ensure shine and purity, and is also scented to leave a fresh scent after use. This liquid is suitable for use on windows, mirrors, and glass furniture, which facilitates the cleaning process and gives great results.

True Soft features

  • True Glass liquid contains an effective formula specially designed to clean glass and transparent surfaces. This formula contributes to the rapid removal of dirt and stains
  • True Glass liquid is characterized by the fact that it does not leave any traces on the glass after use, which makes it an ideal choice for cleaning windows and mirrors. Its effective formulation ensures that the glass remains clean and shiny without any streaks or stains. This makes it easier for you to get professional cleaning results without the need for additional effort
  • True Glass liquid contains a scented formula that gives a refreshing scent during and after its use. This feature makes the glass cleaning experience more enjoyable, as the benefits are not only limited to effective cleaning, but also include leaving a beautiful scent that lasts for a while. The scented formula helps eliminate unwanted odors, ensuring your glass spaces remain clean and fresh.
