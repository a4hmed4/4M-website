What is True Soft?

"True Soft" fabric softener and freshener features an effective, high-quality formula that contains luxurious French perfumes. This formula not only adds softness to clothes, but also gives them a beautiful, long-lasting scent, making clothes more fresh and attractive. The “True Soft” formula helps reduce wrinkles, which facilitates the ironing process and makes clothes look tidy.
